Story summary

Here is a summary of the story from fandom.com:

"The story follows a young boy named Gon Freecss, who was told all his life
that both his parents were dead. But when he learns from Kite, an apprentice
of his father Ging Freecss, that he is still alive and has since become an
accomplished Hunter, Gon leaves his home on Whale Island to take the Hunter
Exam in order to become a Hunter like him."

Favourite Character

Kurapika:

"Kurapika is the last survivor of the Kurta Clan. He is a Blacklist Hunter and
the current underboss of the organization founded by Light Nostrade. He is a
member of the Zodiacs with the codename "Rat". His goal is to avenge his clan
and recover the remaining Scarlet Eyes." Source

Gon:

"Gon Freecss is a Rookie Hunter and the son of Ging Freecss. Finding his father
is Gon's motivation in becoming a Hunter. He has been the main protagonist for
most of the series, having said role in the Hunter Exam, Heavens Arena, Greed
Island, and Chimera Ant arcs. He was also the deuteragonist of the Yorknew
City arc." Source
